## Install & Use (3 Steps)

1. **Install Extension**
   - Chrome → `chrome://extensions/` → Developer mode ON
   - Load unpacked → Select extension folder
   - Extension loads automatically

2. **Navigate & Export**
   - Go to: https://www.creditkarma.com/networth/transactions
   - Click extension icon
   - Choose preset OR enter dates: `YYYY-MM-DD` format (e.g., `2024-10-01` to `2024-10-31`)
   - ✅ Enable "Strict boundaries"
   - Click Export

3. **Wait & Verify**
   - Wait for completion (3-25 min depending on range)
   - CSV downloads automatically
   - Check: Should have start and end date transactions

## Available Presets

| Preset | Date Range | Time | Transactions | Status |
|--------|-----------|------|--------------|--------|
| **This Month** | Current month | 3-5 min | ~50-60 | ✅ PRISTINE |
| **Last Month** | Previous month | 3-5 min | ~130-140 | ✅ PRISTINE |
| **Last Year** | Previous full year | 15-25 min | ~700-800 | ✅ Working |
| **Last 2 Years** | Nov 19, 2023 - Nov 18, 2025 | 18-20 min | ~2,286 | ✅ PRISTINE |
| **Last 3 Years** | Nov 1, 2022 - Nov 18, 2025 | 22-25 min | ~2,865 | ✅ PRISTINE |

**Maximum Working Range**: **3 years** (verified)

---

## Tips

- ✅ Use `YYYY-MM-DD` format (not `MM/DD/YYYY`)
- ✅ Always enable "Strict boundaries"
- ✅ Watch console (F12) for progress
- ✅ Recommended: Single month or year for best results
- ⚠️ Maximum range: 3 years - Split longer ranges into multiple extractions
